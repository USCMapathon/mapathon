USC Map-a-thon

Join us November 15th from 10am to 1:30pm at at the Polymathic Innovation Lab on the 3rd Floor of Leavey Library for a special humanitarian mapa-a-thon event. We will join forces (and compete!) with UCLA as we learn more about OSM (Open Street Map) and work on a project that needs urgent mapping.

When: Wednesday November 15th, 2017

Where: Polymathic Innovation Lab, 3rd Floor of Leavey Library

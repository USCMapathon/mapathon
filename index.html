USC Map-a-thon

Join us November 15th from 10am to 1:30pm at at the Polymathic Innovation Lab on the 3rd Floor of Leavey Library for a special humanitarian map-a-thon event. We will join forces (and compete!) with UCLA as we learn more about Open Street Map, Missing Maps, and work on a project that needs urgent mapping.

When: Wednesday November 15th, 2017
Where: Polymathic Innovation Lab, 3rd Floor of Leavey Library
Food will be provided!!

What to do before the Map-a-thon

Get an OSM account

Watch some videos on how to use the task manager in Open Street Map.

What to expect once the Map-a-thon begins?

We will have a project picked from the HOTOSM website. This project will be in need of mapping.

We will be mapping with (and against) UCLA!

The more you know and have set-up before (see instructions above!) you get here the faster you can get to mapping. We will be giving brief tutorials/workshops to get people up to speed and we will have volunteers on hand to get you started and answer questions

As we map we will have a leaderboard showing our progress against UCLA. In addition we will have a live feed so we can say hello and see how are counterparts are doing
